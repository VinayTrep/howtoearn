Plan Details

To give the affiliates a motivation to earn more quickly we use a 3*3 matrix compensation plan.

Another powerful feature of our compensation plan is that you get a 200% sponsor’s commission for all the members that you personally sponsor and 300% sponsors match commission for the non-sponsored members. Which helps you earn quickly.

In brief:-

You get to earn in 3 streams of income lines.
  • Instant commission
  • Sponsors match
  • Sponsors indirect match

You earn a one-time instant commission per member and this depends on the row in which they are in.you earn a commission when:

  • A member in row #1 purchases product#1 – you earn ₹50
  • A member in row #2 purchases product#2 – you earn ₹100
  • A member in row #3 purchases product#3 – you earn ₹150

You earn a sponsor’s match commission whenever a person you directly sponsored makes a sale. You earn a sponsor match commission when:

  • Your personally sponsored member earns a product #1 instant commission of ₹50 then you earn a sponsor’s match commission of ₹100.
  • Your personally sponsored member earns a product #2 instant commission of ₹100 then you earn a sponsor’s match commission of ₹200.
  • Your personally sponsored member earns a product #3 instant commission of ₹150 then you earn a sponsor’s match commission of ₹300.

You earn a sponsor’s indirect match commission whenever a person you directly sponsored makes a sale. You earn a sponsor indirect match commission when:

  • The person in your Row#2 earns a product #1 instant commission of ₹50 then you earn a sponsor’s indirect match commission of ₹150.
  • The person in your Row#2 earns a product #2 instant commission of ₹100 then you earn a sponsor’s indirect match commission of ₹300.
  • The person in your Row#2 earns a product #3 instant commission of ₹150 then you earn a sponsor’s indirect match commission of ₹450.

Disclaimer: - All the Income streams shown below is just for theoretical purpose only. Affiliates can earn even more or less depending on their effort and the way of their understanding. Make sure you fully understand the instant, sponsors match and sponsors indirect match commission pay plans. A downline team member joining at level one on your row three will not earn you a commission, but could possibly earn you a matching commission.
